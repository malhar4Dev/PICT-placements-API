Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the common questions about our placement platform

🎯

How Does the Placement API Integration Work?

Our platform integrates with PICT's Placement API to provide real-time updates on drives, student applications, and recruiter interactions. This ensures students have access to the most current information.

💼

What Information is Available About Companies?

Our platform provides comprehensive data on companies visiting the college, including the year of the visit, skills required, selection process details, and LPA offered.

📊

How is Student Selection Data Displayed?

Data on the number of male and female students selected, as well as branch-wise selection, is presented in interactive graphical form with filters for better analysis and clarity.

📈

Can I View Year-Wise Placement Trends?

Yes, you can explore company visit and placement trends year by year through our interactive visualizations. This helps identify patterns and growth in recruitment over time.

🎓

How Can This Data Help Students?

Students can identify trends in company requirements, popular skills, and branch-wise placement patterns to better prepare for future drives and make informed career decisions.

🔐

Who Can Access This Data?

The platform is accessible to students, faculty, and recruiters seeking detailed insights into campus placements. Different access levels ensure appropriate information sharing.
